## 📐 **Technical Specifications**

### **Glassmorphism Implementation**
```css
backdrop-filter: blur(25px) saturate(180%);
-webkit-backdrop-filter: blur(25px) saturate(180%);
```
- Applied to navbar, buttons, cards
- Creates frosted glass effect
- Modern iOS/macOS aesthetic

### **Shadow Strategy**
- **Light elements**: 3-4 shadow layers
- **Outer shadows**: for depth
- **Mid shadows**: for softness
- **Inset shadows**: for highlights
- **Colored shadows**: match brand (gold/pink/green)

### **Transform Philosophy**
- **Hover states**: translateY(-4px to -10px) + scale(1.02-1.05)
- **3D effects**: rotateX(5deg) for menu cards
- **Preserve-3d**: where applicable for true 3D
- **Smooth cubic-bezier** easing functions

### **Animation Timing**
- **Fast interactions**: 0.3s for immediate feedback
- **Standard**: 0.5s for smooth transitions
- **Slow**: 0.8s for dramatic reveals
- **Bounce**: cubic-bezier(0.68, -0.55, 0.265, 1.55)
